SBI VC Trade approved Ripple's RLUSD stablecoin for trading in Japan on 24 June 2026, the country's first foreign-issued stablecoin cleared under the Payment Services Act. Only the Ethereum version qualified, not the token on Ripple's own XRP Ledger.
