Baillie Gifford launched the Baillie Gifford Enhanced Yield Fund, the United Kingdom's first fully native tokenised fund, on the Ethereum and Solana blockchains. BNY provides the tokenisation and wallet infrastructure, with NatWest acting as depositary.
